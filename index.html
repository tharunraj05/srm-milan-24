"National-Level Cultural Festival organized by the Directorate of Student Affairs of SRM Institute of Science and Technology"

Milan is the annual national-level cultural festival organised by the Directorate of Student Affairs of SRM Institute of Science and Technology. With a footfall of about 70,000 students, it is one of the most successful cultural fests in the nation today. The four days cultural extravaganza extensively spans multiple varieties of events, which include music, dance, dramatics, and creative arts, just to name a few. Each year, in addition to these events, Milan also organises extravagant pro shows, live concerts, music bands, and international DJ performances.
